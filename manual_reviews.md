## Review Framework

The following factors were considered during manual review:

1. Revenue Contribution
2. Purchase Frequency
3. Recency
4. Support Ticket Activity
5. Return Behavior
6. Campaign Engagement
7. Website Activity

## Business Recommendation

Manual review should primarily focus on:

* High Value Unhappy customers
* At Risk customers
* High-spending customers showing declining engagement

These customers represent the greatest potential revenue impact if churn occurs.
